About

The Vintage Pitch©

I am a music lover and wanted to create a website where I could post my thoughts on English music from the 1920's through the 1980's and share it with the world. Building The Vintage Pitch© from scratch gave me a wonderful opportunity to learn more about the Django web framework while also sharing my views on vintage music. Now you could too, and I hope you enjoy the site!

The Developer
